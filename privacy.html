Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

Hair Docs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website at hairdocs.com (the "Site"). Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information through this website. The types of information we may collect include:

  • Information you provide voluntarily — such as your name and phone number if you contact us directly by phone to book an appointment.
  • Automatically collected information — when you visit our Site, standard web server logs may record your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit. This information is used solely for maintaining the security and performance of our Site.

We do not operate any online booking forms, user accounts, or payment processing systems on this website. We do not collect sensitive personal information such as credit card numbers, Social Security numbers, or health information through this Site.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information collected through or in connection with this Site is used only to:

  • Respond to your inquiries or appointment requests
  • Operate and improve the functionality of our website
  • Ensure the security of our Site

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our Site may use basic cookies or similar technologies to support site functionality (such as remembering your navigation preferences). We do not use cookies to track you across other websites or for advertising purposes. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, though this may affect some features of the Site.

4. Third-Party Links and Services

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites such as Google Maps, Facebook, Yelp, and others. Once you leave our Site, this Privacy Policy no longer applies. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information associated with this Site. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of any information.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Choices

Since we collect very limited personal information through this Site, there is little data to manage. If you have shared your contact information with us by phone and wish to request that we no longer contact you, please call us directly at 630-226-6899.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date. Your continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
