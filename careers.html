Why Work With Us

Family Atmosphere

Loyal Client Base

Our clients keep coming back. You'll build lasting relationships with people who trust and value your skills.

Flexible Scheduling

We believe in work-life balance. Enjoy a schedule that works for your lifestyle and keeps you energized.

Great Location

Quality Products

Work with premium hair care products that make your job easier and keep clients coming back for more.

Women-Owned & Proud

What We're Looking For

We take pride in delivering exceptional results. That means we need stylists who do too.

Proven Experience

You should have solid, hands-on experience cutting, styling, and working with a variety of hair types and textures.

Coloring Experience

Experience with hair coloring is required — whether it's all-over color, root touch-ups, highlights, or balayage, we need stylists who are confident with color.

Skill & Confidence

You know what you're doing behind the chair. From precision cuts to creative color, you deliver consistent, quality results.

Client-First Attitude

You genuinely care about making every client feel heard, comfortable, and thrilled with their look.

Reliable & Professional

You show up on time, communicate clearly, and take your role seriously — while still having fun doing it.

Licensed Cosmetologist

A valid Illinois cosmetology license is required to join our team.
